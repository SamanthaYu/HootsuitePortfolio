Hello World, I'm Samantha.

When I came across the "hello, world" program in "The C Programming Language" (also known as "K&R") by Brian W. Kernighan and Dennis M. Ritchie, I was amazed. This was the original "hello, world". Although printing a short message to the screen is not inherently exciting, I was intrigued how it has influenced computer science and created a tradition in itself.

Traditionally, the first program in a new language is "hello, world". I appreciate its simplicity. It represents how much I have learned since my first "hello, world" and the beginning of a new adventure.

In this adventure, I am eagerly exploring the world of technology. I began this journey with only logic, creativity, and determination on hand. Although I have tried different programming languages, worked on various projects, and learned some algorithms, I continue to rely on the foundations of logic, creativity, and determination.
